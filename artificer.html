Function

Masters of invention, artificers use ingenuity and magic to unlock extraordinary capabilities in objects. They see magic as a complex system waiting to be decoded and then harnessed in their spells and inventions.

Artificers use a variety of tools to channel their arcane power. To cast a spell, an artificer might use alchemist's supplies to create a potent elixir, calligrapher's supplies to inscribe a sigil of power, or tinker's tools to craft a temporary charm. The magic of artificers is tied to their tools and their talents, and few other characters can produce the right tool for a job as well as an artificer.

Stats

Health Points

Hit Dice: 1d8 per artificer level

Hit Dice

How many times you roll this die when regaining health from a short rest

Hit Points at 1st Level: 8 + your Constitution modifier

Hit Points at 1st Level

How many health points you get on the 1st level

Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per artificer level after 1st

Hit Points at Higher Levels

Your level is the amount of times you roll a 1d8 + Constitution modifier to determine your max hitpoints

Proficiencies

Armor: Light, medium and shields

Armor

You can won't be disadvantaged on ability checks, saving throws or attack rolls involving Dexterity or Stength, plus you can use spells even with these equipped.

Weapons: Simple weapons

Weapons

When attacking, you add your proficiency bonus to your attack roll.

Tools: Thieves’ tools, tinker’s tools, one type of artisan’s tools of your choice

Tools

When doing an ability check with these tools, you add your proficiency bonus to your roll.

Saving Throws: Constitution, Intelligence

Saving Throws

When your hitpoints have reached 0 you have to make some saving throws to stableize yourself. These are the stats you can choose to do saving throws from.

Skills: Choose two from Arcana, History, Investigation, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Sleight of Hand

Skills

You add a proficiency bonus when making checks involving your chosen skills.

Equipment

In addition to the equipment you get from your background, you start with:

  • Two chosen simple weapons
  • Light crossbow and 20 bolts
  • studded leather armor or scale mail
  • Thieves’ tools and a dungeoneer’s pack

Studded Leather

Valued at 45gp and gives 12 + Dex Modifier AC.

Scale Mail

Valued at 50gp and gives 14 + Dex Modifier (max 2) AC and stealth disadvantage.

Alternatively, you can start with 5d4x10 starting gold
